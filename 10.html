Kusama, Y. (1990s). Pumpkin. [Sculpture installation]. Art

Yayoi Kusama’s pumpkin sculptures embody her obsession with repetition, polka dots, and the dissolution of self. They’ve become pop-cultural phenomena and Instagram icons, yet originate from deeply personal experiences of hallucination and infinity. Her work blurs the lines between avant-garde, therapy, and spectacle.
