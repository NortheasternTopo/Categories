Di Rita, M. (2021). University of Padua [graffiti].

This mural by Italian graffiti artist Peeta (Manuel Di Rita) at the University of Padua masterfully manipulates form to create the illusion of sculptural volume on a flat architectural surface. Through precise shading, perspective, and curvilinear shapes, the work transforms the building façade into a dynamic interplay of spatial depth—demonstrating how form can visually disrupt and redefine the built environment..
