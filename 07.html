Müller-Brockmann, J. (1953). Poster for the Zurich Tonhalle. [Offset print]. Art / Design

A prime example of Swiss Style, this poster demonstrates grid-based design, sans-serif typography, and minimalist aesthetics. It reflects the movement’s aspirations toward objectivity, universality, and clarity. The style became a foundation for modernist graphic design, especially in institutional and corporate settings.
