Childish Gambino. (2018). This is America. [Music video still]. Directed by Hiro Murai. Art / Propaganda

“This is America” uses stark, violent imagery and layered symbolism to critique gun violence, racial injustice, and media distraction in contemporary America. The choreography and setting juxtapose entertainment and trauma, forcing audiences to confront uncomfortable truths through the language of viral culture.
