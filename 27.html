Edwards, B., Pratt, H., & Freleng, F. (1963). The Pink Panther [Animation still].

The Pink Panther exemplifies the use of flat planes in cel animation, where bold, unshaded color areas define form without relying on depth or texture. This playful visual economy—built on clean lines, stylized shapes, and saturated hues—demonstrates how flat planes can convey personality, dynamism, and narrative clarity with striking simplicity.
