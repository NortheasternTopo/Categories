van Gogh, V. (1889). The Starry Night. [Oil on canvas]. Museum of Modern Art, New York. Art

Van Gogh’s swirling, expressive brushstrokes convey an internal emotional world as much as a visual one. “The Starry Night” is celebrated not just for its aesthetic beauty, but also for its capacity to express turbulence, longing, and spiritual reflection. It’s a hallmark of how personal experience shapes visual representation.
