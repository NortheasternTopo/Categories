Moholy-Nagy, L. (1927). A 19 [Oil and graphite on canvas].

László Moholy-Nagy’s A 19exemplifies the plane as a foundational element of visual communication through its bold geometric forms and spatial precision, reflecting Bauhaus ideals of clarity, structure, and modernity. By manipulating flat shapes with light and shadow, Moholy-Nagy transforms the two-dimensional surface into an exploration of depth and movement, illustrating the plane's power to convey complex visual ideas in both art and design.
